How to Enjoy Hokkaido

The Great Outdoors
Many beautiful national parks, lakes, and mountains that are perfect for hiking, camping, and other outdoor activities.
Local Cuisine
Known for its delicious seafood, dairy products, and vegetables. 'Miso Ramen', 'Soup Curry', 'Jingisukan' and more are originated in Hokkaido.
Japanese Onsen (Hot Springs)
Natural hot springs, offering relaxing and soaking experiences, in the therapeutic mineral waters. Some of the most popular 'Onsen' are in Hokkaido.
Festivals
Hosting many festivals throughout the year. These events are a great way to experience local culture and have fun.
Cities
The cities have a lot to offer, shopping, nightlife, and cultural attractions. Be sure to explore the local neighborhoods and try the local cuisine.

Public Transport

Train
JR Hokkaido:
Covering major cities such as Sapporo, Hakodate, and Asahikawa, as well as smaller towns.
Known for its scenic train routes, offering panoramic views of Hokkaido's natural beauty.
The Official Website

Subway
The Sapporo Municipal Subway:
Subway systems in Hokkaido are limited to the city of Sapporo.
If your destinations are in the city, this is one of the best way to get to the places.

Bus
